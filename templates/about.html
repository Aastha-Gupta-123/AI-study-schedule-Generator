About AI Study Schedule Generator

Empowering students with AI-driven study planning

What We Do

AI Study Schedule Generator is an intelligent platform that uses machine learning algorithms to create personalized and optimized study schedules. Our system analyzes your subjects, their difficulty levels, and your available study time to generate the most efficient learning plan.

How It Works

Data-Driven Approach

Our AI model is trained on extensive study data to understand optimal time allocation patterns.

Machine Learning

Random Forest algorithms predict the optimal study hours for each subject based on difficulty.

Smart Scheduling

Intelligent time slot distribution ensures balanced learning across all subjects.

Sentiment Analysis

Analyze your study feedback to understand patterns and improve your learning experience.

Key Features

AI-powered schedule generation
Support for multiple subjects (up to 6)
Difficulty-based time allocation
Customizable study hours and duration
Time slot optimization (Morning/Afternoon/Evening)
Sentiment analysis for study feedback
User-friendly interface
Responsive design for all devices
